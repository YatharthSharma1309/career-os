# Job Search Engine — Launch Guide

## Resume variants

| ID | File | Use when |
|----|------|----------|
| A | `resume-variant-a-fullstack-ai.md` | JD mentions AI, LLM, RAG, GenAI |
| B | `resume-variant-b-react.md` | JD emphasizes React, Next.js, frontend-heavy full-stack |
| C | `resume-variant-c-associate.md` | Junior / associate / trainee titles |

Log variant in `job-tracker.csv` column `resume_variant`.

## Weekly targets

| Week | Applications | Referrals | Follow-ups |
|------|-------------|-----------|------------|
| 1 | 50+ | 5 | 10 |
| 2–4 | 50 | 5 | 10 |
| 5–12 | 35–40 | 3 | 10 |

## Daily checklist

1. [ ] 10–12 tailored applications (70% India, 30% global remote)
2. [ ] Log each in `job-tracker.csv`
3. [ ] 1–2 follow-ups on apps from 5–7 days ago
4. [ ] 5 LinkedIn comments on eng leaders / hiring posts
5. [ ] Update status column when replies arrive

## Channel tracking

In `job-tracker.csv`, use consistent `channel` values:

- `LinkedIn`
- `Naukri`
- `Instahyre`
- `Wellfound`
- `Company-site`
- `Referral`

Review every Sunday: which channel has best reply rate?

## Interview mode trigger

When **≥1 interview scheduled**, shift afternoon block to prep (`05-interview-prep/`).

When **≥3 active interviews**, prep becomes 35% of day per master plan.
