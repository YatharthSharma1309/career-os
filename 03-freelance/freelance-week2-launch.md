# Freelance Backup — Week 2+ Launch

**Start date:** Jul 6, 2026 (after live demos)  
**Volume:** 5 personalized outreaches/week  
**Floor:** ₹15,000–25,000 per MVP-scope project

## Prerequisites

- [ ] RecruitAI live URL + Loom recorded
- [ ] SupportAI live URL + Loom recorded
- [ ] `03-freelance/packages.md` reviewed
- [ ] Contra or Upwork profile created (secondary channel)

## Weekly workflow

| Day | Action |
|-----|--------|
| Tue | Identify 3 prospects (LinkedIn, Wellfound, Twitter/X founders) |
| Wed | Send 2 DMs with Loom + demo link (use proposal templates) |
| Fri | Send 3 more + follow up on non-responders from prior week |
| Sun | Log in `freelance-tracker.csv` |

## ICP (ideal customer profile)

1. Indian SaaS startup (5–30 employees) with support scaling pain
2. Recruiting agency or HR tech exploring AI screening
3. B2B startup needing CRM MVP before hiring full team

## Escalation (if no FT interviews by Week 6)

- Increase to 10 outreaches/week
- Lower floor to ₹15,000 fixed-scope only
- Offer 1-week "AI chatbot integration" sprint using SupportAI fork

## Track in freelance-tracker.csv

Columns: prospect, company, channel, package, date, status, follow-up, call, amount_inr, notes
