# RecruitAI — Client Pitch

**Use for:** HR agencies, recruiting firms, freelance proposals

## Elevator (30 sec)

Manual resume screening costs hours per role and produces inconsistent shortlists. RecruitAI automates first-pass screening: upload resumes, get scores against your job description, skill gaps, and suggested interview questions — so your team focuses on humans, not PDFs.

## What to emphasize

| They care about | Your proof |
|-----------------|------------|
| **ROI** | Cut first-pass screening from hours to minutes per batch |
| **Time saved** | Batch upload + one-click analyze per candidate |
| **Business outcomes** | Consistent scoring criteria tied to JD, not reviewer mood |
| **Communication** | Clear dashboard — pipeline statuses, notes, audit trail |

## Package (from [packages.md](../packages.md))

**Resume Screening Tool** — ₹15,000–25,000 fixed, 1–2 weeks

**Deliverables:**

- Job and candidate management
- PDF/DOCX parsing + AI match score
- Skill gap + interview question output
- Deployed app (Vercel) with your branding

**Timeline:** Week 1 — core flow; Week 2 — polish + deploy + handoff

## FAQ snippets

**Q: Is candidate data secure?**  
A: Resumes stored in private storage (Supabase bucket in production); LLM keys server-side only.

**Q: Which file formats?**  
A: PDF and DOCX, with manual text fallback.

**Q: Can we use our own job descriptions?**  
A: Yes — each job has its own JD for scoring.
