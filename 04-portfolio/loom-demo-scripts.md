1. **Hook (10s):** "RecruitAI helps recruiters screen resumes in minutes, not hours."

3. **Upload (20s):** Upload a sample PDF resume → show parsed candidate row.
4. **Analyze (25s):** Click Analyze → show match score, missing skills, summary.
5. **Pipeline (15s):** Shortlist candidate → show status change.
6. **Close (5s):** "Built with Next.js, Prisma, and OpenRouter. Link in description."
