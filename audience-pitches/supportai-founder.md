# SupportAI — Founder Pitch

**Use for:** SaaS founders, "we need support AI yesterday"

## Elevator (30 sec)

Your users ask the same questions repeatedly. SupportAI lets you upload FAQs/docs, embed a chat widget on your site, and get grounded answers with citations — plus agent handoff when AI can't resolve it. I shipped the full loop: ingest → chat → escalate → analytics.
