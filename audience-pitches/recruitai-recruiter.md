# RecruitAI — Recruiter Pitch

**Use for:** job applications, recruiter DMs, interview "tell me about a project"

## Elevator (30 sec)

I built RecruitAI — a recruiter dashboard that parses PDF/DOCX resumes, scores candidates against a job description, surfaces skill gaps, and generates interview questions. It's full-stack Next.js with Prisma, server-side LLM integration, and production-ready auth/storage hooks.
